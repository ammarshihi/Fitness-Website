Ultimate Fat Loss Weekly Plan

Your Goal: Calorie Deficit & High Protein

This plan is strictly designed to promote fat loss by sustaining a moderate calorie deficit. We prioritize lean protein and fibrous vegetables to maximize satiety and preserve muscle mass while minimizing unnecessary sugars and high-calorie fats.

Daily Target Calories: 1800 kcal (Adjust based on BMR)

Protein: 40% | Carbs: 35% | Fat: 25%
