Body Recomposition Programs

Combining strength training and conditioning to build muscle while simultaneously reducing fat.

Hybrid Strength & Conditioning

Main Lifts (Strength First)

Romanian Deadlift (RDL)

Builds hamstring and glute size with moderate load.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Benefits: Focuses on muscle development over max strength.

Barbell Overhead Press

Develops strong, defined shoulders and core stability.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Benefits: Excellent for shoulder aesthetics and strength.

Dumbbell Goblet Squat

A full-body squat variation focusing on technique and core.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Benefits: High-volume quad/glute work with low spinal load.

Metabolic Conditioners (Fat Loss Focus)

Push-Up / Dumbbell Press Super-Set

Combines bodyweight and light weight for high endurance burn.

Sets/Reps: 3 rounds, Max Push-Ups then 10 DB Press

Benefits: Builds muscle endurance in the chest and arms.

Barbell Hip Thrusts

Prioritizes glute and hamstring muscle growth.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Benefits: The best isolation movement for glute development.

Battle Ropes (HIIT Finisher)

A high-demand cardio finisher to burn maximum calories.

Sets/Reps: 5 rounds of 30 seconds work / 30 seconds rest

Benefits: Shreds fat and builds shoulder/core endurance.
