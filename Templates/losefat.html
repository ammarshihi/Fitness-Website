Fat Loss Programs

High-intensity, high-volume routines designed to maximize calorie burn and shred fat.

Full Body & High-Intensity Circuits

Cardio & Metabolic

Burpees

Explosive full-body movement for peak calorie expenditure.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (or 60 seconds)

Benefits: Boosts metabolism and cardiovascular fitness.

Mountain Climbers

Intense core and total body engagement.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 45-60 seconds

Benefits: High-speed cardio with core focus.

Kettlebell Swings

A powerful full-body metabolic finisher.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 20 reps

Benefits: Targets hips, glutes, hamstrings, and shoulders.

Toning & Endurance

High-Rep Walking Lunges

High-volume work to tone legs and improve endurance.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 20 steps per leg

Benefits: Improves functional fitness and muscle definition.

Dumbbell Rows

Maintains back muscle and improves posture during fat loss.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 15 reps per side

Benefits: Balances pushing movements and tones the back.

Box Jumps (Plyometrics)

Explosive leg exercise for maximum power output.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Benefits: Great for burning calories and improving explosiveness.
