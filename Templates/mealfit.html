Stay Fit Weekly Meal Plan

Your Goal: Maintain & Energize

This plan is designed for balanced nutrition, aiming for steady energy levels throughout your day while helping you maintain your current fitness level. Focus on lean protein, complex carbs, and plenty of vegetables.

Protein: 30% | Carbs: 45% | Fat: 25%
