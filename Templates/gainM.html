Upper Body Builders

Barbell Bench Press

The fundamental chest, shoulders, and triceps builder.

Weighted Pull-Ups

Intense back, biceps, and core development.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 5-10 reps (max effort)

Benefits: Adds width and thickness to the back.

Barbell Bicep Curls

Direct isolation for peak arm growth.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Benefits: Hypertrophy focus on biceps peak.

Lower Body & Power

Heavy Barbell Squat

The ultimate exercise for building leg mass.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 5-8 reps

Conventional Deadlift

Maximal posterior chain and full-body power.

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 3-5 reps

Benefits: Increases density and raw strength.

High-intensity isolation for calf hypertrophy.

Sets/Reps: 4 sets of 15-20 reps

Benefits: Target the gastrocnemius muscle.
